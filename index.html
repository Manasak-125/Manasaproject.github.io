About Me

Everywhere you look, there is nothing but data.

A single website click, or even just viewing or commenting on a picture or video, can produce a massive amount of data.

Isn't it interesting to use this data to generate some insightful conclusions and support the business in making data-driven decisions that would boost their growth and profitability!

This interest and curiosity made me to choose data analysis as a career option.

As a self-starter, I have acquired all the fundamental skills required to become a data analyst, from comprehending the business issue to using the data to create a compelling story.

Additionally, I developed my proficiency with data analysis tools, including R, Python, Tableau, SQL, and Microsoft Excel.I'am excited and looking forward to apply all my skills and knowledge to the real world business challenges and help the business make data driven decisions.

Project Portfolio

Super Store Sales Analysis

This article goes into great detail on the insights that were found from analyzing sales data from superstore sales data.
I used the six-step data analysis process—Ask, Prepare, Process, Analyze, Visualize, Act—to finish this project.

Tools Used:

  • Kaggle :Data Source
  • Ms-Excel :Data exploration and Data Cleaning
  • Tableau :Data Visualization

Ask

Initial step in the data analysis process,understanding the problem statement to be solved.

Prepare

This step involves data gathering using kaggle and also data management.

process

This step involves data exploration and data cleaning using MS-excel.

Analyze

Analyzing the data to uncover necessary information anad insights required to answer the business problem.

Visualize

Building an interactive dashboard to help clients and stakeholders understand and comprehend the results.

Act

Putting all the uncovered insights to work to solve the business problem.
